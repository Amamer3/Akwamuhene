NANA YAW BARIMA TWUM SARKODIE I
THE ASONA CLAN

Akwamuhene of Akyem Awisa

The meaning of Awisa given at the time when the said family were cultivating guinea grains at the hills of Wisabere, near Asene. This Agona family came from Dnekyera a town Mmereyemu which means that (good; nothing hard for). Some tradition says that this town was not the town for the family as thought of but a certain town at Ashante called Ayesem was their proper town. When they were at Ayesem, their Kings were the people at Dweso who bear the name Agona family.
